Care plans explained

Some parts of our care plans are self explanatory but some might fly over your head so below we’ve explained each one for you.

Hosting

This is where your website is stored and will affect:

  • The speed of your website
  • The security of your website
  • The up-time/reliability of your website
Backups

It is important that the website is backed up. Regardless of which care plan you choose, you will get free monthly backups for the life of the website.

Content Updates

This is the process of keeping the wording and images up to date. In almost all cases we build our websites so that you and your staff can make these changes.

Security

All websites are probed and attacked on a constant basis. It is important to take into account security to ensure the website is not hacked or attacked. Website security is the process of scanning the website and fixing any vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

Software Updates

Website technology changes constantly. In order to ensure the website stays secure and working on the latest hardware it is important to allow the software that powers the website to update.

Support

Depending on the care plan you choose we have different options for support. With our support options we are on hand by phone and email to assist when you have questions or problems.
