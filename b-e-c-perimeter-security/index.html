B.E.C Perimeter Security

B.E.C Perimeter Security came to us in need of a completely new website to replace their previous HTML website. Established in 1981, B.E.C provide a wide range of products and services relating to perimeter security.

THE BRIEF

B.E.C Perimeter Security came to us in need of a brand new website. Their previous website was built completely out of HTML and had a very out of date look. It was important that all products/services should be seen upon the homepage and visitors should be able to contact at all times.
